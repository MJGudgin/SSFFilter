Setup:
	Download the Public.filter from the github repository
	Move the .filter file to your 'my games/path of exile' directory
	Swap to Public.filter in the in-game UI options

Editing:
	Chancing Bases
		Uncomment Chancing Bases section and add the bases you're looking for after 'BaseType' - Lines 557-567

	Unique Highlighting
		Uncomment Uniques section and add the bases you're looking for after 'BaseType' - Lines 805-815

	Chaos Recipe
		Chaos Recipe can be toggled on by changing line 1451 to Show instead of Hide
	
	
This filter is rather simple and should easy enough to tweak to your preferences.
